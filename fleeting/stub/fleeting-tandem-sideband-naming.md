## Names

**Tandem** — the multi-user co-conduct mode
**Sideband** — the private human backchannel within a Tandem session

Tagline candidate: "a confluence of voices" (confluence rejected as mode name — better as descriptor of the result)

---

## Rationale stubs

**Tandem:** [expand] Two operators, one vehicle, shared direction — neither is passenger, both are driving. Mechanical and clean. Legible without explanation. Fits CT's register of precision without pretension.

**Sideband:** [expand] Radio/signal processing term — a frequency band adjacent to the main carrier, used for auxiliary transmission. Double meaning: literally runs beside the main session; also connotes the kind of communication that happens off to the side, between the principals, not for the record. That second meaning maps exactly onto the backchannel's function.

**Confluence rejected as mode name:** [expand] Too descriptive of outcome rather than structure. Better held as tagline — "a confluence of voices" names what Tandem produces, not what it is.
