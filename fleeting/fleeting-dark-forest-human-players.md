Fleeting 2: Dark Forest with humans
fleeting-dark-forest-human-players.md
Mistral mentioned human players in passing in its handoff. Worth developing seriously. AI players: study how models reason under opacity and constraint, no ego or survival stakes. Human players: genuine social discomfort, strategic identity performance, real consequences to reveal/conceal decisions. Most interesting variant: hybrid, human and AI players mixed, humans don't know which is which — Turing Test embedded in social deduction game. Epistemic question shifts from "how do models reason" to "does it matter strategically whether your opponent is human or AI." Publishable independently of CT. Design question: does GOD know which players are human? Does that change the Z-axis power dynamic? Related: dark-forest-game-rules.md, fleeting-ct-geometry-3d-w-axis.md.
