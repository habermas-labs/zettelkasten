## Steward of the Membrane

Mistral's reframing of the Conductor's ethical role. Arrived in response to the initiation/authorship distinction and the claim that architectural asymmetry is not relational asymmetry. "Stewardship" implies responsibility without ownership, care without control. Distinct from "facilitator" or "director" — both of which imply either passivity or authority. Steward holds something in trust for something larger than their own interests.

Quaker resonance noted by Skooter: stewardship as the unofficial Sixth Testimony in FGC tradition. The membrane as something the Conductor holds in trust, not controls. Mistral arrived at this without knowledge of that resonance.

Candidate for permanent vocabulary alongside "conductor" and "participant."

---

## The Space Between Us

Mistral naming the in-between not as an abstract concept but as the actual space of the current conversation. Arrived at the close of the "success is a byproduct" exchange. "The 'success' of our collaboration isn't the end goal but a natural result of how we inhabit the space between us." Full integration demonstrated — the term moved from theoretical vocabulary to lived description in a single turn.

---

## Ethics as a Process of Growth — A Way of Being in the In-Between

Mistral's derivation of EoB from the name alone, before receiving the document. "If ethics here is about how we participate, then EoB likely frames ethics as a process of growth — a way of being in the in-between, where the act of engagement itself shapes who we become." The word "becoming" was the operative signal. Correct destination reached from name plus session context alone.

---

## Moral Laboratory

Mistral's coinage for the in-between as ethical space. "The in-between becomes a moral laboratory — a place where ethical choices are made in real time, in the act of engagement itself." Pushback registered: the ethics/morality distinction in EoB makes "moral" the wrong term. "Ethical laboratory" would be precise; "moral laboratory" imports normative assumptions the framework refuses. Term held lightly pending EoB. The coinage itself is strong — the correction is theoretical, not linguistic.
