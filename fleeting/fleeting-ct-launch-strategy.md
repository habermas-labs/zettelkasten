Raw capture from walking session. Not settled thinking — generative surface for future development.

- **Ko-fi as launch platform:** Hybrid one-time + recurring model fits CT's staged needs. Lower fees than Kickstarter, no all-or-nothing pressure. Early adopters can support without committing to subscription; ongoing access via recurring tier.
- **BYOK as default at launch:** Avoids key distribution logistics and cost-risk while platform is unproven. Ko-fi support tier for development funding only — no fulfillment obligation at this stage.
- **Eventual proxy model:** Cloudflare Workers architecture already positions CT well for user-transparent API proxying once subscription infrastructure (Stripe) is in place. Not a day-one concern.
- **API credit programs:** Anthropic, Google have developer/startup credit programs worth investigating before any capital raise. CT is a credible novel use case on both platforms.
- **Acquisition as long horizon:** Not yet thought through. The theoretical framework + educator/researcher user base + genuine paradigm differentiation ("multi-model as epistemic practice" vs. single-model race) are the acquisition appeal. Big three model providers and productivity tools (Perplexity-type) both plausible acquirers eventually.
- **Income constraint:** 10-month salary contract ends June. July-August gap if staying in teaching. Soft deadline for having something public-facing.
- **Plain-language explainer as separate project:** Pedagogical arc is clear — compressed heteroglossia → different training → different strengths → use all three → get something none provides alone. Hook: CT as answer to "AI makes us intellectually lazy" anxiety. Format TBD (conversational/first-person voice preferred over polished product copy).
