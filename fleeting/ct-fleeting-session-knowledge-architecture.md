## The Core Reframe

CT's export system is not a feature set. It is a theoretically-grounded knowledge practice. The shift from "export as utility" to "export as epistemic act" is the load-bearing move.

---

## Session Volatility as Design Principle

Session volatility is a feature, not a bug. Everything generated in a CT session disappears unless the conductor chooses to preserve it. This is correct. Not everything generated in a generative session deserves to survive. The act of deciding what to carry forward — and in what form — is itself a theoretical claim about the value and shape of what was built.

Contrast: single-model history is a flat chronological list optimized for retrieval. It answers "what did we talk about" but not "what did we build, and how do I build further from it." The naming problem in native interfaces is a symptom: when history has no structure, naming is the only organizing principle, and it is fragile.

---

## Export as Spectrum of Intentionality

The export ecosystem spans a spectrum from minimal to maximal intentionality:

- **Plain text / full transcript** — archival, faithful, cognitively inert without further structure
- **Bullet summary / narrative summary** — synthetic, requires interpretive choice about what mattered
- **Focus Thread** — curatorial, live annotation at the moment of sharpest attention; the most intentional act during the session itself
- **Exit scaffold** — forward-looking, re-entry artifact; captures not what happened but the conditions for productive continuation

Each format is a different answer to: what needs to be carried forward, and in what form?

---

## Connection to Workload Outsourcing

The export decision is precisely the kind of judgment that does not transfer to the model. The conductor decides what persists. Models generate; the conductor curates. This asymmetry is the point — and it is the same asymmetry that distinguishes workload outsourcing (labor transfers, judgment stays) from cognitive offloading (judgment risks transferring too).

---

## Connection to the In-Between

Session volatility enables epistemic discipline in the same way the In-Between enables productive tension. You are not forced to resolve the session into a permanent artifact. You can let most of it go and carry forward only what earned survival. That is not loss — it is the practice working correctly.

---

## Connection to Ethics of Becoming

The export choice is an ethical act in the EoB sense: a moment of authorship that constitutes the self in relation to the knowledge generated. What you choose to preserve and how you frame it is who you are becoming as a thinker. The tool does not make this choice for you — the tool makes the choice structurally available and its character legible.
