## What this archaeology established

The six threads are the theoretical infrastructure built alongside CATR — not a side project but the philosophical grounding that gave the pedagogical work its depth. The lineage runs: CATR as practical problem → theoretical development to ground it → manual triangulation to test multi-model friction → CT as the tool that operationalizes the whole thing.

Almost nothing was abandoned. What changed across the period is depth, precision, and integration — things got named more carefully, connected more rigorously, grounded in more specific theoretical touchstones. The shape of the project was visible very early.

## What was left behind and has recovery value

**Emergence model** — Pascal's triangle / W = ΣP + ΣI equation / apparent-hidden mirroring / Moravec connection. Substantial original work sitting only in an article draft and chat logs. Not represented in ZK. Most significant underdeveloped thread from this archaeology.

**Befindlichkeit / attunement** — Heidegger's pre-cognitive disposition to a situation as the frame for comfort-with-discomfort. Confirmed as tracking in the hermeneutics thread but absent from the-in-between.md. Clean, small gap to fill.

**Diagonal instability hypothesis** — matched participation modes (especially generative-generative) as potentially unstable for learning; asymmetric pairings as producing productive tension. Appeared in participation modes thread, not carried forward. Has design implications for Tetra role structure.

**Orientation (dispositional) / intention (volitional) distinction** — Chat's reframe in the participation modes thread. Not formally integrated into participation-gigo-matrices.md. Relevant to modality-within-mode and to CT session design.

**The spiral observation** — drift causing the hermeneutic circle to become a spiral; the return always to different ground. Compact and precise. Not formally treated in any ZK note.

**Being nourished vs. having/acquiring** — raised in the Wu Wei thread, deferred, never picked up. Maps directly onto vnenakhodimost' / positionlessness-as-resource but from a Taoist angle. Complementary rather than redundant framing.

**CATR vocabulary** — Cognitive Apprenticeship through Tool Reasoning canonical terms list confirmed. Not yet integrated into CT ZK work. Identified as the origin project from which CT emerged.

**The "me, you, us" dialogic dimension** — the ongoing constitution of the "us" through dialogue (Bakhtinian) versus the emergent product (emergence model). Both dimensions needed; only the emergence dimension was fully drawn out in the metacognition thread.

**Cognitive exoskeleton / living metaphor** — scaffold critique is sharp; exoskeleton captures molting quality but is inaccessible. Living metaphor question (hermit crab shells, snake skin, language structures for developing child) remains open. Relevant to ZPD discussion in CT educational context.
