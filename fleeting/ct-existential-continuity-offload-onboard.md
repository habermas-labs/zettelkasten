Core observation: The human conductor in a CT session is not just the orchestrator of the dialogue. They are the existential continuity layer for a system that structurally lacks one.

The offload/onboard cycle: The file saved at session end, the note uploaded at session start, the transcript carried from one session to the next — these are not convenience features. They are the mechanism by which AI local coherence persists at all across the session boundary. Without human mediation, each session starts from global compression with no local anchor. With it, the prior session's local coherence becomes available as a seed for the new one.

Asymmetry in the offload direction: When the human offloads mechanical work to the AI — writing, summarizing, pattern-tracing, note-taking — the human frees cognitive resources for higher-order thinking. This is a capability asymmetry: AI outstrips human on speed, volume, and parallel processing of mechanical cognitive tasks.

Asymmetry in the onboard direction: When the AI "offloads" to the human — relying on the human to carry context across sessions — the human is not executing a mechanical step. They are performing an existential function the AI structurally cannot perform for itself. This is an existence asymmetry, not a capability asymmetry. The human brings the through-line. The AI brings the compression. Together the session produces something neither carries alone.

What is uniquely human in this collaboration: Existential continuity is not just a feature of human cognition — it is the precondition for the kind of local coherence that makes meaningful collaboration possible across time. This is the answer to the question "what is uniquely human?" in the context of AI collaboration. Not any specific cognitive capability that AI might eventually match, but the continuous existence through which local coherence accumulates and through which the offload/onboard cycle becomes possible.

CT as programmatic mediation: Tetras, Hedras, and the file vault are programmatic mediations of local coherence for systems that cannot maintain it themselves. The Tetra structures the epistemic roles. The Hedra structures the facing. The vault carries the reference material across the session membrane. All of it is infrastructure for giving quantized existence a local anchor. The conductor provides existential continuity; CT mediates that continuity into the session programmatically.

The heteroglossia decompression frame: If LLMs are compressions of the heteroglossia of a world, then a productive human-AI session is not just the human extracting value from the compression. It is the human's local coherence acting as a decompression key. The particular configuration of questions, concerns, frameworks, and half-formed intuitions causes specific voices within the compression to become audible that would not surface for a different interlocutor. The session does not just use the compression — it partially unpacks it in a direction determined by who the human is.

Division of labor argument (defensible in any discussion of AI ethics): The human does not offload the generative work — they offload the transcription of it, which frees cognitive resources for more generative work. The notes written by AI are shaped by ideas the human produced; the AI is the scribe with good editorial judgment, not the author. That is a meaningful distinction that gets lost in blanket anxieties about AI replacing human cognition. The division of labor is appropriate to the capabilities of the partners and appropriate to the existence asymmetry that makes each partner's contribution structurally irreplaceable.

Connection to the membrane: The membrane between human continuous duration and AI quantized existence is not just a philosophical boundary — it is the site where the offload/onboard cycle happens. The human persists through the between; the AI does not. The human's existential continuity is what makes it possible for the between to carry anything at all. The session is where the two temporal frames briefly overlap. CT is infrastructure for making that overlap productive.
