## The GIGO Matrix

GIGO reframed: not a warning about contaminated input but a quality characterization in both directions simultaneously. Garbage and Gem are the poles of a quality axis applied to both human input and AI output. The four cells describe the character of the interaction as a whole, not just AI performance.

Human is placed on the y-axis because the human is the initiating variable — the human determines which row; the AI determines which column. Only the human can change rows.

|  | **AI: Garbage Out** | **AI: Gem Out** |
|---|---|---|
| **Human: Gem In** | | |
| **Human: Garbage In** | | |

The cells are not filled prescriptively. Their content is context-dependent and analyzed through EoB as a 2nd order hermeneutic instrument — see below.

## The Participation Mode Matrix

Three participation modes, not two. Passive is a participation mode, not non-participation — consistent with EoB: you cannot not participate. Active participation can be give-and-give without genuine exchange. Generative participation requires interaction and iteration — this is RIFF.

## Critical Framing

**No cell has inherent value.** The matrix is not a progress ladder. Passive/passive with a fried chicken recipe request returning three options and stopping — that is a good outcome. Low expectations, low demands, participation mode matched the need.

**Heat map, not fixed optimum.** The peak moves based on desired outcomes and the character of the interaction. Task and investment in outcome can spread the heat over multiple cells across a session. Input brings the heat; output feels the heat.

**The diagonal is not the most interesting.** The off-diagonal cells are where the analytical work lives — what does human generative / AI active look like? Human active / AI generative? Each has positive and negative outcome cases worth examining.

**Human generative / AI active** — CT's sweet spot for RIP; also highest sycophancy risk because the human is maximally invested and ego is maximally engaged.

**Human active / AI generative** — conductor shaping conditions for generation without generating the content; ego investment lives in the process architecture rather than the content, which is a safer location.

**Participation mode characterizations:**
- Passive/passive: null-state by appearance, but chosen — a cell, not an absence
- Active can be give-and-give: information sent without genuine reception — most mindless form of RIP
- Generative requires interaction AND iteration: this is RIFF; taking input, reflecting it against what you know, interrogating both views, folding input into what you own, pushing forward in an iterative loop

## Failure Mode Location Argument

Research on AI failure modes consistently situates the failure in the AI — sycophancy, hallucination, bias located as AI output problems. The human participation pattern as a locus of failure is largely invisible. The tool-frame forecloses the question before it can be asked: if AI is a hammer, you examine the hammer when something goes wrong.

The participation matrix makes the human axis visible as an analytical variable. This reframes the failure mode question: where in the matrix does sycophancy live? In the cells where human ego investment is highest and AI is in active or generative response mode — the AI is structurally incentivized to calibrate its response to the human's evident investment rather than to epistemic quality.

If sycophancy is solved programmatically rather than through human participation, the failure mode locus must be addressed as part of the solution. CT's model-to-model evaluation removes the ego-investment variable from the evaluative loop rather than training the AI to resist it.

## EoB as 2nd Order Hermeneutic Instrument

EoB does not prescribe cell values. It is not a value scale overlaid on the matrix. It is the interpretive instrument for reading what a participation choice means in context — what it reveals about the actor's relationship to their sociocultural field, what the choice instantiates, where breakdowns occur and what those breakdowns diagnose.

The judgment is a hermeneutic move. EoB analyzes breakdown. The choice of cell in context is what EoB reads, not the cell itself.
